**Last Updated: March 12, 2026**

## 1. Information Collection and Use

### No Data Collection
The WhatsApp Message Backup Viewer is designed to process your WhatsApp chat backups **locally on your device**. 
*   **No Personal Data:** We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal data, including names, phone numbers, or chat history contents, to any external servers.
*   **No Analytics/Tracking:** We do not use third-party analytics or tracking tools to monitor your usage of the App.

### Permissions
The App may request access to your device's file system or document picker to allow you to select and view your WhatsApp chat backup files. These files are read only for the purpose of displaying them within the App and are never uploaded or shared outside your device without your explicit action (e.g., using the Export function).

## 2. Information Disclosure
